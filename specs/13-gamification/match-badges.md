## Badge Visibility

### In Open Market

Matches with badges are:

- Highlighted visually
- Optionally sorted higher
- Filterable (show only Ready to Play, etc.)

### In Invitations

Badge appears on match invitation cards.

### In Calendar

Badge icon appears on calendar events.
