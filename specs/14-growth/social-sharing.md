## Facebook Group Auto-Post (Future)

> **Growth Hack:** Identify tennis/pickleball Facebook groups and offer to post matches automatically.

### Flow

1. User creates public match
2. Suggest relevant Facebook groups
3. User authorizes posting
4. Match posted to group (with user's account)

### Group Identification

Research and maintain list of:

- Local tennis groups
- Local pickleball groups
- Active groups per geographic area
